What is Tastespace?

Think about the last time you tried to find a new place to eat dinner. You had to scroll through a list of restaurants you’ve never been to and hope you’ll find an item you’d like on the menu. This process only becomes more and more difficult the more people you bring with you.

We’d like to speed up this dining decision making process by introducing TasteSpace. TasteSpace is a mobile app that finds the best food recommendations for you at any restaurant based on a database of food relationships and user preferences. TasteSpace learns your preferences and helps you:

1) choose what restaurant you should go to using its recommendation platform
2) select what restaurant to go to that satisfies you and your friends' preferences
3) figure out what to eat once you get there.
